Use Cases by Segment

Defense & Government ($15B TAM)

  • Pain: Classified data cannot touch cloud
  • ARIA Solution: DoD compliance mode, air-gap operation, secure code generation
  • Customer Profile: Defense contractors, federal agencies, military
  • Example: Classified network AI for mission planning and code development

Healthcare ($30B TAM)

  • Pain: HIPAA violations carry massive fines
  • ARIA Solution: Zero PHI exposure, on-premise deployment, medical transcription
  • Customer Profile: Hospitals, clinics, medical practices, pharma R&D
  • Example: Patient note automation without HIPAA risk

Legal Services ($12B TAM)

  • Pain: Attorney-client privilege must be absolute
  • ARIA Solution: No third-party data sharing, contract analysis, deposition transcription
  • Customer Profile: Law firms, corporate legal departments
  • Example: Contract review and drafting with zero disclosure risk

Financial Services ($40B TAM)

  • Pain: SOC 2, PCI-DSS compliance critical
  • ARIA Solution: On-premise deployment, trading algorithm development, risk analysis
  • Customer Profile: Banks, hedge funds, fintech
  • Example: Trading strategy development without IP exposure

Manufacturing R&D ($25B TAM)

  • Pain: IP theft would destroy competitive advantage
  • ARIA Solution: Local-only processing, CAD/CAM automation, quality control
  • Customer Profile: Automotive, aerospace, consumer goods
  • Example: Product design with complete IP protection

Aviation & Maritime ($18B TAM)

  • Pain: In-flight/at-sea = no internet
  • ARIA Solution: 100% offline capability, maintenance docs, crew training
  • Customer Profile: Airlines, shipping companies, cruise lines
  • Example: In-flight operations support without connectivity
